Industrial Monitoring

To keep you in the loop.

Pressure Monitoring

As demand in the manufacturing industry for extensive and continuous operation increases, it is vital to integrate a safe and reliable industrial monitoring system.

More and more factories rely on reliable pressure monitoring system sensors to maintain and efficiently control any industrial operation. Real-time sensor data ensures industries are better able to understand and maintain their processes. Incorporating such data into the IIoT also aids in the optimization of raw material ordering, handling, and consumption. Knowing what to get and when to order it can help keep continuous processes running smoothly. Our pressure monitoring system accurately measures pressure of any gas or liquid medium while also being one of the safest and most reliable systems available.

Computer Vision

AI-enabled computer vision technologies replace many of inefficient labor-intensive operations for greater reliability, safety, and efficiency. By deploying AI smart cameras in manufacturing, you can automate your production line and improve quality control for packaging inspection, reading barcodes, defect reduction and many more applications.

Tank Level Monitoring

Proper monitoring and management of tank level and volume increases an industry’s profitability and productivity. The level of any material stored in any tank can be checked from any location at any time using an IoT-powered tank monitoring system. You will be able to successfully manage inventory with the help of our solutions. The real-time visibility alerts you to the remaining days until stock runs out and prevent theft.

Chemical Gas detection

Gas detection systems based on Internet of Things can be easily integrated into a company’s existing systems and equipment, allowing for real-time detection of gas leaks that could cause a significant disaster. As a result, immediate action can be taken to not only prevent further spread of gas over a large area, but also to locate and correct the source of the leak.
