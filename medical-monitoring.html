Medical Monitoring

Air Quality Monitoring

Healthy indoor air quality is vital in medical care facilities such as hospitals, healthcare centers, nursing homes, assisted living and operating rooms. Autonera’s technologies offers a modern way to help hospitals meet indoor air quality standards. Our solutions for indoor air quality provide you greater control and monitoring over your environment remotely. Sensors placed in various areas of your facility are capable of measuring anything from CO2 and VOCs to carbon monoxide, dust particles etc. in real time. This data can then be analyzed and interpreted, allowing you to formulate a plan of action to restore a healthy indoor air quality. You can also immediately detect, for instance, a potentially damaging leakage in one area of the hospital and isolate it to minimize the impact on air quality elsewhere.

Room Pressure Monitoring

Room Pressure Monitors (also known as Room Pressure Indicators) and Environmental Monitors are intended to keep essential pressurized locations such as hospital isolation rooms, operating rooms, pharmacies, and cleanrooms safe by limiting the possibility of contamination. Autonera’s environmental monitors/controllers and room pressure monitors are exceptionally reliable and precise.

Blood Banks Monitoring

Blood products must be recorded on a regular basis. When done manually, it is a time-consuming and labor-intensive operation for staff. With an automated solution, hospitals can avoid expensive mistakes, product waste, and insufficient blood supply.

The environmental monitoring system from Autonera safeguards the integrity of your blood bank inventory. Our comprehensive solution includes dashboards displaying current and historical data, notifications and alerts when predefined parameters are exceeded, as well as robust software applications. The solution also automatically creates compliance reports and consistently detects temperature and humidity levels and differential air pressure throughout the blood storage process.

Pharmaceutical Refrigerators/Freezers Monitoring

Temperature monitoring in medical refrigerators and ultra-cold freezers must be error-free. Autonera’s provides a range of systems designed to fulfil your demands, whether you have a consumer-grade unit, a pharmaceutical refrigerator, or a deep freeze unit.

Our solutions provide a continuous monitoring system that operates round the clock, that provides real-time notification through e-mail, SMS, or phone. This ensures that a team member is alerted the instant a system goes into alarm, providing them enough time to appropriately handle the problem and react in a way that saves the product involved. No more manual checks for temperature readings or the risk of missing due to an outage.

Medical device integration with hospital BMS/HL7

In hospitals, Building Management Systems (BMS) are provided to be connected with all types of mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire protection and security systems for their smooth operations.

All of our solutions and services are integrable with your existing BMS. Our experienced engineers are also capable of integrating your current infrastructure into your BMS.
